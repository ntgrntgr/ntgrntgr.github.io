Sunny Day in Winter

Take some time off, go outside - embrace the sun! I am a plant, I need to do photosynthesis! Melt all this snow off my branches, I long to see them clothed in leaves again. I am an early bird, your light guides me, therefore I fly towards tomorrow. I am a night owl, I define my identity in your absence - Shine!Remind me to get some rest.
